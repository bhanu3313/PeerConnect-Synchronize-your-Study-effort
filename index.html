About PeerConnect

PeerConnect helps you synchronize your study efforts by connecting you with study buddies and providing virtual study rooms.

  • Once logged in, navigate to the Dashboard to see your study buddies and available study rooms.
  • Join a study room of your choice and start collaborating!
